    plant, (kingdom Plantae), any multicellular eukaryotic life-form characterized by photosynthetic nutrition.in which chemical energy is produced from water, minerals, and carbon dioxide with the aid of pigments and the radiant energy of the Sun,essentially unlimited growth at localized regions.

    Introduction

    Plants range in size from diminutive duckweeds only a few millimetres in length to the giant sequoias of California that reach 90 metres (300 feet) or more in height.

    definition

    The kingdom Plantae includes organisms that range in size from tiny mosses to giant trees
